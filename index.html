About PeerQuest Tavern

Learn more about our platform and how it works.

Our Story

PeerQuest Tavern was founded in 2023 by a group of adventurers who wanted to create a better way for skilled individuals to connect and collaborate. Inspired by fantasy role-playing games, we built a platform that makes skill-sharing fun and rewarding.

Our mission is to help adventurers of all skill levels find meaningful projects to work on, build their reputations, and form lasting collaborations through our guild system.

How It Works

  1. Create an adventurer profile showcasing your skills
  2. Browse quests posted by other adventurers or guilds
  3. Apply for quests that match your skills and interests
  4. Complete quests to earn gold, XP, and reputation
  5. Join or create guilds to collaborate on larger projects
